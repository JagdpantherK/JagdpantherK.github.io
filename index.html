Bio

According to Parade, when Sulik Bot, aka "Homeless", was 15 years old, he was alone. He survived near the combined heat and power plant until he met the alien Wise Nordic. He went with him on a three-year space trip After parting with Nordic, Sulik went to Neri. Where later he graduated from the highest academy of martial arts of the planet Neri and received the fifth degree of warrior (the highest). Currently, Sulik is serving in the personal guards of Emperor Ruslan.
